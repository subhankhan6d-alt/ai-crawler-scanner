FAQ

Is this free?

Yes. Every check pulls from public data (robots.txt, llms.txt, page HTML) — no paid API, no signup, no card.

What exactly does the score measure?

Whether major AI crawlers are blocked in robots.txt, whether llms.txt exists, whether structured data (schema) is present, whether basic meta tags are set, and whether the page has real content without needing JavaScript to render.

Is this an official spec validator?

No — these are heuristic checks meant to flag obvious problems quickly. Always spot-check anything important manually before acting on it.

How often is the AI bot list updated?

The list is current as of mid-2026. New AI crawlers appear periodically, so treat this as a snapshot, not a permanent spec.
